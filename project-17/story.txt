The Foolish Old Man Who Moved Mountains

Long ago, an old man named Yu Gong lived with his family between two enormous mountains, Taihang and Wangwu. These mountains blocked their way, forcing them to walk miles around them just to reach the other side. One day, Yu Gong gathered his family and announced, "I have decided to dig away these mountains so we can have a straight path forward."

His neighbors laughed. Even his wife said, "You are almost ninety years old. How can you move something so huge?" Yu Gong smiled. "If I cannot finish it, my sons will. If my sons cannot finish it, my grandsons will. The mountains will not grow taller, but our effort will never stop."

Yu Gong and his family began digging. They carried baskets of earth and stones to the distant sea day after day. A wise man called Zhi Sou saw this and mocked him, "You are so foolish! How can a lifetime be enough to move two mountains?" Yu Gong replied calmly, "You are the foolish one. When I die, my children and their children will continue. Generations will persist until the mountains are gone."

The God of the Heavens heard of Yu Gong's determination and was deeply moved. He sent two mighty gods to carry the mountains away on their shoulders. Overnight, the obstacles vanished. The story teaches that perseverance and faith can overcome even the most impossible challenges.
